WhatsApp Chatbot

An automated helper that makes guest support and bookings easier.

WhatsApp concierge for guests

Nifty helps hotels respond to guests on WhatsApp. It handles common questions, helps capture booking details and suggests local places — and it can pass things to staff when a human is needed.

Key Features

Friendly replies

Keeps short conversation context so replies feel natural across a few messages.

WhatsApp messaging

Sends and receives messages, photos and contact details right inside WhatsApp.

Booking help

Collects booking details and can send the information to your reservation system or notify staff to follow up.

Photo gallery

Send curated photos and room galleries on demand.

Local suggestions

Quickly turn guest requests into map links and nearby suggestions.

Easy staff handoff

Notifies staff with a short summary and shares a contact card so a team member can take over smoothly.

How it works

1. Guest sends a message: A guest messages the hotel on WhatsApp and may send text, photos or documents.

2. System decides: The system interprets the message and decides whether to reply, send images, give a map link, capture booking details, or ask a staff member to step in.

3. Actions are taken: The system sends the chosen response or notifies staff to follow up, and can hand off the conversation with a short summary.

4. Short context is kept: A short recent message history is kept to make follow-up replies smoother.

How it connects

The system links WhatsApp to the hotel's tools and stores a small amount of recent context and image references so it can reply quickly:

  • Connects to WhatsApp for messaging and media.
  • Uses a reliable language service to help form replies and decide actions.
  • Keeps short recent conversation history and image references for context.
  • Optionally connects to a bookings system to submit reservation details.
  • Notifies staff via WhatsApp when human help is needed.

Privacy & data handling

Only a short recent conversation history is kept and media is stored by reference. Booking details are passed to the hotel's own system rather than kept in long-term logs, and staff notifications use a short summary to protect guest privacy.

Results

Faster replies: Common questions get answered in seconds instead of hours.

Less routine work: Automating simple tasks freed staff to focus on more important guest needs.
