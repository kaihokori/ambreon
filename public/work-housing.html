Housing Analysis

Clear charts and summaries that explain housing costs and market trends.

Simple, visual housing insights

Housing Analysis turns raw housing data into clear visuals and summaries that show trends in prices, supply and affordability. Reports focus on overall patterns and keep individual details private.

Overview

This includes bringing data together, cleaning it up, making easy-to- read charts and producing summary reports. We only share grouped results so individual people or households are not identified.

Core pieces

Data pipelines

Tools to collect and tidy data from different sources so it's consistent and ready to analyse.

Visualisations

Clear charts and maps that show trends in prices, supply and affordability.

Aggregated reporting

Grouped summaries and dashboards suitable for briefings and easy review.

Approach

  • Apply clear steps to clean and standardise data from different sources.
  • Share only aggregated results so individual records stay private.
  • Provide documented files that show how analyses were done for easy review and reuse.

How it's done

Data tools
