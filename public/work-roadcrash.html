Road Crash Investigation

Tools that review dash-cam video and highlight key moments to help explain incidents.

Video review and clear incident timelines

The system examines dash-cam video to find important moments, lines them up with available sensor or GPS data, and creates annotated clips and timelines that make the sequence of events easy to follow.

What we do

  • Pull out key frames from dash-cam footage and spot important moments like sudden braking or near-misses.
  • Align the video with GPS or other sensor logs when available to build a clear timeline.
  • Create annotated clips and short summaries that explain what happened in plain terms.

Capabilities

Event detection

Automatic video checks that find near-misses, sudden moves and objects appearing on the road.

Timeline building

Lines up video, GPS and other sensor info to create a single clear timeline of events.

Reporting

Export annotated video clips and simple PDF summaries ready for review.

Privacy & usage

Video is handled with privacy in mind: faces and license plates can be blurred in exports and raw footage is kept in secure storage with restricted access. Aggregated, anonymous workflows are supported for larger studies.

How it works

Video processing

Extracts frames and looks for motion and objects in the road.

Data alignment

Combines video with GPS and sensor logs to make timelines more precise.

Exports

Produces annotated clips, event summaries and simple CSVs for review.

Interested?

We can adapt the tools for fleet-wide analysis, work with partner teams, or provide simple tooling for forensic review. Contact us to talk about your footage and needs.
