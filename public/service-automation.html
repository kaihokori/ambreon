FAQ

How do you handle sensitive data for AI?

We apply tenant isolation, encryption at rest and in transit, and can run AI models in client-managed cloud accounts or on-prem when required. We also support data minimization, anonymization, and secure model access controls for AI workloads.

Which cloud platforms do you work with?

AWS, GCP, Azure and hybrid environments. With AI tooling and managed model services as appropriate. We recommend best-fit AI tooling per project.
